As it is written in Jeremiah 29:11:
“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.”

I admire your intellect at such a young age. One of the first things that drew me to you was your wisdom; at 17, I could have easily believed you were 22 because of the depth in your words. Your intelligence challenges me and inspires me to be better.

You are ambitious, determined, and beautiful inside and out. I could not be prouder of the future you are building. You are a powerful source of motivation in my life.

And your beauty—oh, your beauty. Those alluring eyes, that perfect smile, the way you articulate your thoughts, your understanding beyond your years, your care, your spiritual depth. I love you so deeply that English sometimes falls short.

Mathematically, you are an 11/10. Imagine a 30-question True/False test: the chance of getting every answer wrong is 1 in 1,073,741,824. That’s how rare and extraordinary you are.

And if Economics is the study of value and scarcity, then by supply and demand, you are priceless. There has never been—and will never be—another you. You are a limited edition in a world of mass production.

Leonardo da Vinci once said, “Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.” That’s you: effortless, extraordinary, perfect in every detail.

In physics, imagination turns possibility into reality. I imagined you before I even knew you existed, imagined being with you, holding you, sharing a future together. And now, every glance, every smile, every thought of yours is like the perfect equation: precise, effortless, and breathtakingly beautiful.

I cherish you beyond measure. I love you in ways English cannot fully express.

Grow in grace, my friend, my best friend, my love, my Blossom. Happy Birthday, My Blossom ❤️
